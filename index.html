Pioneers in Education
Since 1893

To perpetuate the memory of their high priest Sardar Dastur Nosherwan Jamasp, the Parsi Community raised subscriptions to establish Sardar Dastur Nosherwan Girls High School in 1893 at House # 317, Main street, Pune. Later on being presented Bungalow No 3, Arsenal Road (now Moledina Road) by the brothers of the high priest, the school shifted to its present location in 1911, to accommodate a larger number of children, the school underwent expansion when a new building was evicted by the three surviving brothers in the memory of their sister Bai Behmanbai Pestonji Kanga.

Subsequently, to commemorated the Shams-ul-Ulema Sardar Khan Bahadur Dr. Hoshang Jamasp Dastur, MA Phd, CIE Sardar Dastur Hoshang Boys High School was inaugurated on 8 Jan 1912.

Our Schools

Dastur Primary

Bai Najamai Nosherwan Dastur Primary School is a recognized co-educational Primary School founded on 21st September 1953 and is a charitable minority institution under the CHARITABLE MINORITY TRUST. It admits pupils without any distinction of caste and creed, in classes from Pre-Nursery to Std. IV.

Dastur Girls

The Sardar Dastur Nosherwan Girls' High School was founded on 1st February 1893. It is a Charitable Minority Institution under a Charitable Minority Trust. It is a recognised Secondary School and admits girls without distinction of caste and creed. The students are prepared for the Secondary School Certificate Examination.

Dastur COED

Sardar Dastur Hormazdiar High School is a recognized co-educational Secondary School which was founded on 24th February, 1998 by the Founder President, Sardar Dastur Hormazdiar Nosherwan Kaikobad Dastur.

Dastur College

The Sardar Dastur Hormazdiar High School came into being. With the aim of giving children a comprehensive, sound, thorough and all- round education at the school level, The Sardar Dastur School Trust established the Sardar Dastur Hormazdiar Junior College in 2006.
