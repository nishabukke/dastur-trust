Behaviour Guidelines

  • Students are expected to maintain a high stadnard in their behaviour and manner of speech. Their actions and language should be courteous at all times.
  • Students must converse with each other in English in the school premises. They should greet peers, teachers and visitors whenever they meet them.
  • Attentiveness in class and regularity in home assignments is expected.
  • Each student should take pride in keeping the school campus clean.
  • Fighting, bullying, denying use of facilities  to others, damaging school property and causing injury to others will invite strict disciplinary action. All unresolved disagreements among peers must be brought to the class teacher's notice.
  • Students are responsible to the school authorities not only not for their conduct in schools but also for their general behaviour outside. Any objectionable conduct reported or observed bis liable to disciplinary action.

Attendance Policy

  • No Student may remain absent from School without leave. Leave will only be granted on a written application from Parents / Guardians and that too for valid reasons. Leave for attending weddings and other such ceremonies is highly deprecated. Such leave must be entered in the Leave & Absence Record in the Calendar and must have been duly signed by the parents as well as the Principal / Supervisor before the leave is taken.
  • Absence without leave or leaving the School premises without the permission of the Principal for any reason whatsoever, is a grave offence deserving severe punishment at the discretion of the Principal.
  • Students will be allowed half day leave only at the discretion of the Principal.
  • Repeated absence without information renders the student liable to being struck off the Rolls.
  • In the case of leave which could not be obtained with prior permission of the Principal, the student on returning to School must produce her Parent’s explanation of her absence in the Leave & Absence Record in the Calendar. Failure to produce such explanation or Medical Certificate may result in the student being sent home during the class hours in which case the School disclaims all responsibility.
  • If a Student is absent due to illness for more than three days, the Principal must be informed at once.
  • Absence of more than 7 days at any time, without prior intimation, will result in sending a warning letter to the parent.
  • All are expected to attend School on re-opening day after each vacation. Non availability of train / plane reservation will not be accepted as a reason for coming late.

School / College Schedule

  • Schools/College function from Monday to Friday. On Saturdays Schools/College remain closed except for Sardar Dastur Nosherwan Girls' High School and Sardar Dastur Hoshang Boys' High School, which presently  function on alternate Saturdays. Details timings are available on Schools/College websites.
