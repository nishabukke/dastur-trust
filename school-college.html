Faculty & Staff

Faculty and staff of Dastur Institutions are drawn from the cream of academia. The Institutions are ably supported by extremely dedicated teaching and non teaching staff who facilitate honing and optimizing each student’s capabilities and moulding their personalities.

Our committed staff has always been known to understand the students intellectual, physical and emotional needs and through a holistic approach provide an environment to ensure that they grow academically and intellectually. Our teaching staff is continuously exposed to training cadres and workshops to enhance their skills and productivity.

Curriculum & Affiliation

The curriculum at Dastur Schools/College is ever evolving and dynamic with an aim of inspiring a zeal to learn and aquire knowledge through creativity and self expression. Our well thought out curriculums strive to trigger curiosity, instill team spirit and mutual respect for others and their Ideas.

Indoor instruction is complemented by a variety of co-curricular activities which create an ideal blend to equip the students to be successful and socially responsible citizens.

Dastur Schools/College are recognized by the Government of Maharashtra and are affiliated to the State Secondary Schools Certificate and Higher Secondary Schools Certificate Boards.

Infrastructure & Facilities

Dastur Institutions provide state of the art infrastructure for the students which go a long way in creating the right ambience for academic pursuits.

The Schools& College have well lit classrooms, spacious courtyards and open spaces for games and outdoor activities.

The institutions maintain well equipped modern libraries and laboratories to support the instructions in the classrooms.

In 1929, the Trustees/Board of Management was formally established and registered as Charitable Trust to administer and manage the schools.
