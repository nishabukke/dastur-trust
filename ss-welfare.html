Transport & Conveyence

The Schools do not have their own integral transport. A number of Bus operators, however ply their buses to fetch and carry the children from / to schools. Parents are advised to contact the Bus operators plying in their respective areas and follow the guidelines (as given in the Schools calendar) while hiring them.

Schools Transport Committee will facilitate the Transport Agreements between the parents of children and Bus operators and to ensure that all safety parameters prescribed by the RTO/Education Department are implemented.

Counselling & Guidance

The Schools aims to maintain a 'Child Centered Approach’, Child Psychologist/Counsellors are available on the campus to assist the children, parents and teachers to observe, identify and guide the students and parents to cope with problems and work on their strengths and weaknesses.

Group Insurance

Dastur Schools Trust is committed to providing a safe environment for its students. The Students of the institutions are insured against accidents through Students and Staff Safety Insurance Policy.

In 1929, the Trustees/Board of Management was formally established and registered as Charitable Trust to administer and manage the schools.
